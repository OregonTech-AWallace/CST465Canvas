# Internet Culture #5: Macromedia Flash
Before TikTok, before YouTube, there was a company that came up with a game changing technology for the web known as Flash.  Macromedia, which was later purchased by Adobe, developed Flash as a competitor to Java for building lightweight games and animations.  Microsoft tried to compete with a technology known as Silverlight, but it was never able to catch up in popularity.  

Online streaming platforms and advances in HTML/JavaScript allowing for more native creation of games later rendered these technologies obsolete, but I submit for your viewing pleasure one of my favorite Flash movies(which now lives on YouTube ironically):
